Imitate a double clicking mouse.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/76220359/129086672-c2f59164-045d-49f6-a60d-02759aee742f.png)


If you are not getting many double clicks, try increasing the register value from 0.12 to 0.15, or, if you have to hold the mouse button for too long, try lowering it to 0.11.

The CPS cap depends on your physical clicks, a cap of 8 will likely result at 12 to 15 cps.
